Lunch Tray - Solution 
==================================

When the application is first launched by the user, they will be prsented with a screen that contains one button to start the order. 
<br>
<br>
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/57158277/169720653-b6d96fb2-e976-466f-8fb7-9c023edb9cbf.png" width="250">
</p>
<br>
<br>
Once the user clicks on the start order button, they will be displayed with a variety of entree options to select. Each entree contains the ingredients in them so the user can make a better decision. After selecting an entree, the subtotal value will be updated based on their selection. 
<br>
<br>
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/57158277/169720739-5ca1ca82-03fa-40b3-95d2-3f50f2050558.png" width="250">
</p>
<br>
<br>
By clicking on the next button, the user will be shown the sides menu avaliable to order, with a description of the ingredients used. Again, the subtotal will update based on their selection. 
<br>
<br>
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/57158277/169720788-be3bd2d8-1f86-48f5-a3e0-3f5dc1888025.png" width="250">
</p>
<br>
<br>
After choosing a side dish, they will be shown the final selection of food items, the accompaniment menu. Just like the previous screens, each accompaniment contains a description of ingredients, and the subtotal at the bottom will be updated once one is selected. 
<br>
<br>
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/57158277/169720871-12d77636-462f-4844-9552-3462b19361ba.png" width="250">
</p>
<br>
<br>
 Finally, user is shown a summary of the cost of their order, where they can choose to either submit or cancel the order. They are also shown the subtotal of their order before and after tax is accounted for. 
<br>
<br>
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/57158277/169720906-4cbcf083-3d26-4a41-9b73-545be0a9f8cf.png" width="250">
</p>
<br>
<br>
If the user chooses to submit the order, a toast notification is displayed to them saying the order is submitted from the start screen. If they decide to cancel the order, they will simply be redirected to the start screen where they can go through with the process once more.
